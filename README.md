# tscircuit Lab Safety Guidelines

This repository contains safety guidelines and operating policies for equipment in the tscircuit lab. Everyone who operates, assists with, cleans, maintains, or observes lab equipment must follow the applicable policy before work begins.

These documents are intended to prevent injury, fire, property damage, equipment damage, and exposure to hazardous fumes, particulates, hot surfaces, moving parts, sharp tools, chemicals, or electrical hazards.
